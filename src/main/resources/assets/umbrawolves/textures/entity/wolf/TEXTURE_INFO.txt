Wolf Texture Requirements
=========================

Each wolf variant needs 3 textures (48 total for all 16 variants):
- {variant}.png        - Wild/untamed wolf texture
- {variant}_tame.png   - Tamed wolf texture
- {variant}_angry.png  - Angry wolf texture

Texture Dimensions: 64x32 pixels (same as vanilla wolf)

TEXTURE TEMPLATE:
-----------------
Use pale.png as a template. The UV map is:
- Head: 0,0 to 14,14
- Body: 18,14 to 44,32
- Legs: various positions
- Tail: 9,18 to 18,26
- Ears: 16,14 to 20,17

The collar is handled separately by the game (wolf_collar.png from vanilla).
